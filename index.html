• Pocket Business

    Mobility is the future. From facial recognition and contactless payments, to augmented reality; we're finding more and more of what we do now requires a mobile device and smart apps.

    Pocket Business is building a platform for creating native mobile-first retail and service-based e-businesses. It includes customizable workflows, built-in geo-capable marketing, messaging and globally-aware financial accounting, together with dropship product and supply chain integration, and customer-facing apps.

    In short, everything you need in a single wholistic business platform tailored to your specific enterprise - in your pocket.
